About Us

Charity Shop is dedicated to making a positive impact on society by providing support to those in need.

Our mission is to promote social welfare by offering affordable products, supporting charitable causes, and fostering community engagement.

We are committed to transparency, accountability, and integrity in all aspects of our operations.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals, Charity Shop has grown into a trusted and recognized organization.

Over the years, we have expanded our reach and services, thanks to the dedication of our team and the support of our community.

Our Impact

Through our efforts, we have made significant strides in improving food security, promoting sustainable livelihoods, and empowering individuals and families in Pu Ngaol.

Our programs include:

  • Food distribution initiatives targeting vulnerable households, ensuring access to nutritious meals.
  • Training workshops on sustainable farming techniques, empowering farmers to increase crop yields and resilience to climate change.
  • Microfinance programs providing small loans to entrepreneurs, enabling them to start or expand their businesses and generate income.
  • Education and skills development programs for youth, equipping them with the knowledge and tools to pursue employment opportunities.

Furthermore, we measure our impact through regular assessments and feedback from the community, ensuring that our initiatives are making a meaningful difference in people's lives.
